how to make tea

Tea comes with bundles of Health benefits such as boosting of immune system,fight off inflammation and protecting us from every chronic disease

The cup of tea you're drinking may foretell a healthy life ahead

Bellow are the ingredients used and the steps on how to prepare your tea

ingredients

  • sugar/Honey
  • Tea Bags
  • Milk(optional)
  • Water

Preparation

  1. Put a cup of water in a pot until water boils
  2. add milk(optional)
  3. add one or two sachets of tea bags
  4. remove it from the stove
  5. put sugar/Honey
  6. time to have you tea
